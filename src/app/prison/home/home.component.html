Prison System

Description

System developed for the management of a penitentiary, where each of the users can be managed, whether they are guards or directors, and they can manage the prisoners, their entry, location and discharge if they are. It also allows the management of the guards and their information.

Functions

  •     ✅ Directors login
  •     ✅ Wards management
  •     ✅ Reports Viewing
